Strawberry Cheesecake

Description

A rich and creamy classic cheesecake sitting on a buttery graham cracker crust, topped with fresh juicy strawberries. This dessert is a crowd pleaser at any gathering.

It takes a little patience to make but the result is absolutely worth it!

Ingredients

  • 500g cream cheese
  • 150g sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 200g graham crackers
  • 100g melted butter
  • 300g fresh strawberries

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 160°C (320°F).
  2. Crush graham crackers and mix with melted butter, press into the bottom of a springform pan.
  3. Beat cream cheese and sugar together until smooth.
  4. Add eggs one at a time, mixing well after each one.
  5. Add vanilla extract and mix until combined.
  6. Pour filling over the crust and bake for 55 minutes.
  7. Let cool completely then refrigerate for at least 4 hours.
  8. Top with fresh strawberries before serving.
