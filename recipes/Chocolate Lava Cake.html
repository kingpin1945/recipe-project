chocolate lava cake

Description

A warm, rich chocolate cake with a gooey melted chocolate center that flows out when you cut into it. This dessert is perfect for chocolate lovers and is surprisingly easy to make.

Ready in under 30 minutes, this impressive dessert is perfect for dinner parties or a special treat at home.

Ingredients

  • 100g dark chocolate
  • 100g butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 80g sugar
  • 2 tablespoons flour
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 220°C (425°F) and grease your ramekins with butter.
  2. Melt the chocolate and butter together in a bowl over hot water.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk eggs, egg yolks and sugar together until fluffy.
  4. Pour the melted chocolate into the egg mixture and stir well.
  5. Add flour and vanilla extract and mix until combined.
  6. Pour the batter into the ramekins and bake for 12 minutes.
  7. Remove from oven, let sit for 1 minute, then flip onto a plate and serve immediately.
