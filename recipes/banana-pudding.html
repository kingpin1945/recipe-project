Banana Pudding

Description

A comforting and creamy Southern classic made with layers of vanilla pudding, fresh bananas and vanilla wafer cookies. This dessert brings warmth and nostalgia in every bite.

Super easy to make and requires no baking at all. Just layer, chill and enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 4 ripe bananas sliced
  • 2 packs vanilla pudding mix
  • 750ml cold milk
  • 300ml whipped cream
  • 200g vanilla wafer cookies
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Mix vanilla pudding with cold milk and whisk for 2 minutes until thick.
  2. Add vanilla extract and stir to combine.
  3. In a large dish, lay a layer of vanilla wafer cookies on the bottom.
  4. Add a layer of sliced bananas on top of the cookies.
  5. Spread a layer of pudding over the bananas.
  6. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used up.
  7. Top with whipped cream and crushed wafer cookies.
  8. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours before serving.
