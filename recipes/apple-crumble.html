Cinnamon Apple Crumble

Description

A warm and comforting baked dessert filled with soft cinnamon spiced apples and topped with a golden crunchy oat crumble. Best served warm with a scoop of vanilla ice cream!

This is a simple and satisfying dessert that comes together quickly and fills your kitchen with the most amazing smell.

Ingredients

  • 6 large apples peeled and sliced
  • 2 teaspoons cinnamon
  • 100g sugar
  • 150g oats
  • 100g flour
  • 100g brown sugar
  • 120g cold butter cubed

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Peel, core and slice the apples and place them in a baking dish.
  3. Sprinkle cinnamon and sugar over the apples and toss to coat.
  4. In a bowl mix oats, flour and brown sugar together.
  5. Add cold butter and rub it into the oat mixture with your fingers until crumbly.
  6. Spread the crumble topping evenly over the apples.
  7. Bake for 35 minutes until the topping is golden and the apples are soft.
  8. Serve warm with vanilla ice cream or whipped cream.
