Tiramisu

Description

A classic Italian dessert made with layers of coffee soaked ladyfinger biscuits and a rich mascarpone cream. The name literally means "pick me up" in Italian, and this dessert will do exactly that!

No baking required, just mix, layer and refrigerate. Perfect for making ahead of time.

Ingredients

  • 500g mascarpone cheese
  • 4 eggs separated
  • 100g sugar
  • 300ml strong espresso coffee cooled
  • 200g ladyfinger biscuits
  • 3 tablespoons cocoa powder
  • 2 tablespoons dark rum (optional)

Steps

  1. Brew the espresso and let it cool completely. Add rum if using.
  2. Separate the eggs. Beat egg yolks and sugar together until pale and fluffy.
  3. Mix in the mascarpone cheese until smooth.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk egg whites until stiff peaks form.
  5. Gently fold egg whites into the mascarpone mixture.
  6. Quickly dip each ladyfinger into the espresso and layer them in a dish.
  7. Spread half the mascarpone cream over the biscuits.
  8. Repeat with another layer of dipped biscuits and remaining cream.
  9. Dust generously with cocoa powder and refrigerate for at least 4 hours.
